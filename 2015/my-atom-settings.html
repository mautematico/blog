My atom settings

Just in case any of we (because I’m also one of the we set) wish to see my atom.io settings, I have them on a github’s gist.

You can get a copy of my (I hope up to date and complete) relevant configuration files at https://gist.github.com/mautematico/51409ae96652705e33a6

By now the gist is maintained by an atom.io’s plug-in, sync-settings. This plugin gives you the option to backup (upload) your atom settings to a github’s gist. Also it offers you the chance to restore (download) your configuration from the same gist.

This plug-in is not actually a sync tool yet, but I think it’s on it’s own way there… And I hope it’ll be there soon. :simile:

May be I’ll post again about this plugin to show you how to use it. Meanwhile, you can of course give it a look to atom.io sync-settings description, at https://atom.io/packages/sync-settings
